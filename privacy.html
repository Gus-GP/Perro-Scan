Privacy Policy for Perro Scan

Effective Date: September 21, 2025
Last Updated: September 21, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Perro Scan is designed with privacy as our top priority. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information from our users.

Information We DO NOT Collect

Perro Scan does not collect, access, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos or images you analyze
  • Any data that leaves your device

How the App Works

Perro Scan operates entirely on your device using:

  • Local AI Processing: All breed identification happens on your iPhone/iPad
  • Device-Only Storage: All app data remains on your device
  • No Internet Required: Core functionality works completely offline
  • No Servers: We do not operate servers that receive or store your data

About the App

Perro Scan is an AI-powered dog breed identification app that works completely offline on your device for privacy.

How to Use

  1. Take a photo or choose from your library
  2. Get instant breed identification results
  3. View detailed breed information
  4. Share results with friends

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure photos are clear and well-lit
  • The app works best with full-body or head shots of dogs
  • Results are for entertainment and educational purposes

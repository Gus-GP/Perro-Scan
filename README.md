# Privacy Policy for Perro Scan

**Effective Date: September 21, 2025**  
**Last Updated: September 21, 2025**

## Our Privacy Commitment

Perro Scan is designed with privacy as our top priority. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information from our users.** This privacy policy explains our data practices and your rights regarding the Perro Scan mobile application.

## Information We DO NOT Collect

Perro Scan does **not** collect, access, store, or transmit:
- Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
- Device identifiers or unique IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports or diagnostic information
- Photos or images you analyze
- Any data that leaves your device

## How the App Works

Perro Scan operates entirely **on your device** using:
- **Local AI Processing**: All breed identification happens on your iPhone/iPad using built-in machine learning
- **Device-Only Storage**: All app data remains on your device and is not transmitted anywhere
- **No Internet Required**: Core functionality works completely offline
- **No Servers**: We do not operate servers that receive or store your data

## Permissions We Request

Perro Scan requests the following permissions solely for local functionality:

### Camera Access
- **Purpose**: To take photos of dogs for breed identification
- **Data Use**: Photos are processed locally on your device only
- **No Transmission**: Camera data never leaves your device

### Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: To analyze existing dog photos from your photo library
- **Data Use**: Selected photos are processed locally on your device only
- **No Transmission**: Photo library data never leaves your device

### File System Access
- **Purpose**: To save PDF reports and share images you choose to export
- **Data Use**: Only files you explicitly choose to save or share
- **User Control**: You control what gets saved and shared

## Data Processing

All data processing in Perro Scan happens **locally on your device**:
- AI breed identification uses Apple's Core ML framework
- Image analysis uses Apple's Vision framework
- PDF generation happens on-device
- No cloud processing or external AI services

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

- **No Data Sharing**: We do not share any data with third parties
- **No Analytics Services**: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, or similar services
- **No Advertising**: We do not display ads or use advertising networks
- **No Social Media Integration**: While you can share results, we don't integrate with social media platforms' tracking

## Data Retention

Since we don't collect data, there is no data retention:
- **No Data Collection**: We don't collect data to retain
- **Local Storage Only**: Any app preferences are stored locally on your device
- **User Control**: You can delete the app anytime to remove all local data

## Children's Privacy

Perro Scan does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, COPPA regulations do not apply to our data practices.

## International Users

Since Perro Scan processes all data locally on your device and transmits no information, international data transfer regulations (such as GDPR) do not apply to our app's data handling practices.
